How do I buy a car?

Browse cars from the homepage, use filters for make, model, price, fuel type, and location, then press Message Seller to send a private inquiry.

Can buyers see seller phone numbers?

No. Buyer inquiries use private in-app messages. Seller contact details are not shown publicly.

How do I sell a car?

Create an account, open Sell a Car, add vehicle details, upload between 3 and 10 photos, choose a package, then save the listing.

What are the seller packages?

Basic is free, Premium is UGX 30,000/month, and Golden is UGX 40,000. New users also get 2 months of golden free access.

Which payment methods are available?

Premium and Golden can be paid by MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or Visa Card.

Why do I need at least 3 photos?

Clear photos help buyers trust the listing. The upload limit is minimum 3 and maximum 10 photos per car.

How does Google email login work?

Enter your Google email on the Account page and continue. The current demo creates a verified Google-style account for that email.

Where can I manage listings?

Open Dashboard to edit listings, delete listings, update your profile, and view your access/package status.
