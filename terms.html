Terms and Conditions

Rules for using Motor Traders Uganda as a buyer or seller.

Listings

Sellers must post accurate vehicle details, real prices in UGX, and clear photos. Listings may be reported or removed if misleading.

Packages

Basic is free, Premium is UGX 30,000 per month, and Golden is UGX 40,000. New users receive 2 months of golden free access.

Photo rules

Each listing must include at least 3 and no more than 10 vehicle photos.

Buyer safety

Buyers should inspect vehicles, verify documents, and use private messages or support before arranging transactions.

Reports

Users can report suspicious listings for review. False or abusive reports may be ignored.
