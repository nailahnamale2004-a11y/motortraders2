Privacy Policy

How Motor Traders Uganda handles account, listing, payment, message, and support information.

Information we collect

We collect account details, seller profile details, listings, uploaded vehicle photos, saved favorites, reports, package selections, payment method selections, and in-app messages.

Seller contact privacy

Seller phone numbers are not displayed publicly to buyers. Buyers use private in-app messages or Motor Traders support.

Payments

Package payments may use MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or Visa Card. This demo stores selected payment method labels only, not full card or mobile money PIN details.

Photos and listings

Vehicle photos and listing details are used to show cars to buyers and help sellers manage listings in their dashboard.

Account deletion

Users can delete their account from the dashboard. This removes the account, listings, and related private messages from the app storage.
